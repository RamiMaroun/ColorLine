Pre-order

On My Page you can pre-order food on board, and reserve a place in the restaurants. If you travel Oslo-Kiel, you save up to 10% on the buffet and up to 23% on the à la carte when booking in advance. You can also order additional products ashore on My Page.

Do you have a car?

If you need to bring the car on board, you can enter / change the registration number of the vehicle on My page (for easier check-in). On My Page you can also order parking at the terminal in Oslo.
